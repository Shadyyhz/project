About Us

Al Bonyan Company for Engineering and Contracting SARL was established in 1989 (Registration #30919_Lebanon) as a contracting company for construction and development fields in the private and public sectors which include roads, archaeological, infrastructure, municipal services, irrigation, water supply and wastewater; as well as multi-story buildings & their maintenance. Al Bonyan implements the EN ISO 9001:2015 , EN ISO 14001:2015 and ISO 45001:2018 , Quality Management System in all departments, both in back offices and on-site.
